The Greenest Cup Ever.

The EarthCup is a beautiful, functional, reusable take-away cup hand-crafted from bamboo and teak wood. Not only does it carry your favorite beverage in epic green style-- it removes CO2 and plastic from the biosphere.


We carefully consider every element of the EarthCup's life-cycle to make it an ever greener product.

For-Earth
EarthCups.io is a not-for-profit, for-Earth enterprise focused on ecological service. By crafting a product that removes more carbon and more plastic from the biosphere than it generates in its life-cycle, you can be sure that your cup is net-green.

Ecological Accounting
In order to be sure that the EarthCups are in fact net-green, we keep track of our carbon, plastic and biodiversity impacts. We use a GEA Catalyst account to do this and we publish our annual Regenerativity Reports each year.!

Earthen Ethics
EarthCups are an example of a net-green regenerative product as delineated by the theory of Earthen Ethics. This means that we're following the Earth's example of consciousness raising, diveristy supporting, carbon subtracting, benefit sharing in ever facet of our products life-cycle.
